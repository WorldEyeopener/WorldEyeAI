Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 21, 2026
Last Updated: January 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how WorldEyeopener and its affiliates (“WorldEyeopener,” “we,” “us,” “our”) collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you visit our site(s) or interact with our services.

1) Information We Collect

A) Information you provide

  • Name, email, phone number, business name
  • Project details (offer, goals, brand voice, links, FAQs/policies, support scripts)
  • Communications you send (messages, attachments, feedback)

B) Information collected automatically

  • IP address, device type, browser type, operating system
  • Pages viewed, referral URLs, timestamps, general usage data
  • Cookies or similar technologies (see Section 6)

C) Information from third parties

If you engage us via third-party platforms (e.g., Fiverr), we may receive information necessary to fulfill your order, communicate, and deliver services, subject to that platform’s policies.

2) How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve our site(s) and services
  • Respond to inquiries, scope projects, and deliver requested work
  • Send administrative messages (updates, confirmations, service communications)
  • Maintain security, prevent fraud, and enforce our Terms
  • Analyze site performance and improve UX/offer clarity

3) How We Share Information

We may share information:

  • With vendors/service providers (hosting, analytics, email tools) who process data on our behalf
  • With Affiliates for business operations consistent with this Policy
  • To comply with legal obligations (lawful requests, court orders)
  • To protect rights, safety, and integrity of WorldEyeopener, users, or others
  • In connection with a business transfer (merger, acquisition, or asset sale)

We do not sell your personal information in the traditional sense. If your jurisdiction defines “sale” or “sharing” broadly, you may have additional rights (see Section 9).

4) AI Tools and Client Content

For some projects we may use AI-assisted tools to help draft, summarize, test, or structure content. Where third-party AI tools are used, your project materials may be processed only to the extent necessary to deliver services.

Please do not submit sensitive personal data through general website forms, including:

  • Payment card numbers
  • Government ID numbers
  • Medical/health information
  • Biometric data

5) Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for service delivery, business operations, legal/tax/accounting obligations, dispute resolution, and enforcement of agreements. Retention periods may vary by data type and purpose.

6) Cookies and Analytics

We may use cookies and similar technologies to remember preferences, understand site traffic, and improve user experience. You can manage cookies through your browser settings and, where required, via a cookie banner/consent tool.

7) Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8) Children’s Privacy

Our site(s) and services are not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required by local law), and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

9) Your Rights and Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict certain processing of your personal information. To exercise rights, contact us using Section 12.

Note: Some rights depend on jurisdiction (e.g., California CCPA/CPRA, EEA/UK GDPR). If applicable, we’ll respond consistent with local requirements.

10) International Transfers

If you access our site(s) from outside the country where we operate, your information may be transferred and processed in countries with different data protection laws. Where required, we take steps intended to provide appropriate safeguards.

11) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted with a revised “Last Updated” date.
