FAQ

Clear boundaries = fewer revisions.

Do you use AI?
Yes — to accelerate research and iteration — but decisions are human-led: offer clarity, positioning, and final copy/guardrails are crafted deliberately.
What counts as a revision?
Revisions cover changes within the approved brief (tone, clarity, minor restructuring). New offers, new pages, new flows, or extra assets require a package upgrade or add-on via Fiverr.
Can you match my brand voice?
Yes. Paste 2–3 examples of your preferred tone (website copy, emails, or competitors). I’ll mirror vocabulary, cadence, and positioning.
Where does the chatbot “live”?
Basic/Standard can be delivered as a Custom GPT-style assistant with instructions + test suite. Premium includes website chatbot integration plus Zapier/Sheets automation and CRM/DB integration (as scoped).
What do you deliver for copywriting?
Final copy + “Implementation Notes” (how to deploy, what to measure, and A/B test ideas). Deliverables are exactly as listed in your package.
